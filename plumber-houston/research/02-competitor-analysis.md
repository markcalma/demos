## Patterns of the Top 10%

These are the behaviors that separate the top 2 competitors (Abacus, Village) from the rest:

### 1. Massive Review Volume + Active Capture
**What they do:** Village has 9,000+ Google reviews, Abacus has multiple award citations. Both have systematic post-job review request flows.
**Why it matters:** Google Maps pack rankings are dominated by review count + recency. No reviews = invisible in local search.
**Recommendation for Bayou City:** Embed a review request in the post-job follow-up SMS/email. Goal: 50 reviews in 90 days.

### 2. Multi-Path Lead Capture
**What they do:** Phone number (prominent) + online scheduler + offer/coupon form = 3 ways to convert.
**Why it matters:** Different buyers convert differently — urgency converts via phone, planners via form, deal-seekers via offer.
**Recommendation for Bayou City:** Add a "Get Free Estimate" form alongside the phone CTA. Also add a sticky mobile call button.

### 3. Named Trust Signals (Awards, Licenses, Guarantees)
**What they do:** Not just "we're the best" — Abacus lists specific award names and years. Village has a named "Village Guarantee." License numbers are in footer.
**Why it matters:** Vague trust claims are ignored. Specific, named credentials are believed.
**Recommendation for Bayou City:** Name the guarantee ("The Bayou City Promise"). Display TSBPE license number. Pursue Expertise.com, BBB, and Nextdoor Fave badges.

### 4. Heritage or Longevity Anchor
**What they do:** "Since 1946" (Village), "Since 2003" (Abacus), "25+ Years" (Mammoth). Every top competitor anchors themselves in time.
**Why it matters:** Homeowners associate longevity with reliability. New = risky. Old = safe.
**Recommendation for Bayou City:** Counter with specificity — "15 years fixing Houston's toughest jobs" is better than a vague number. Use it in the hero.

### 5. Upfront Flat-Rate Pricing Claim
**What they do:** Village and Abacus both explicitly state flat-rate/upfront pricing with no hidden fees.
**Why it matters:** Pricing anxiety is the #1 friction point for hiring a plumber. Removing it in the headline converts skeptics.
**Recommendation for Bayou City:** This is already in the feature cards but should move to the hero subtitle or a dedicated badge above the headline.
